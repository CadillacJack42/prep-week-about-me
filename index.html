Bio

I enjoy watching movies/shows, reading, listening to music, and learning new things. I am a strong swimmer, but never swim in pools. I love going to concerts big or small, but not actually a fan of being in public.

I have many ideas that I would like to develop in software, as well as in the real world. My ideas are generally about some form of charity. Education

Education, in my opinion, is the single most valueable resource we have on this planet. Education has the power to topple dynasties, while lack of an education can lead to subjugation. Education can feed a person for a lifetime. It is the most powerful tool we have as a society. This is why the most feared nations have extensive intelligence branches, because Knowledge is power.



The London Rae Foundation (LRF)

The LRF is a foundation designed to work towards improving the education experience for students of all backgrounds. Initially this will simply mean providing new clothes for each school year and providing food for all students.
